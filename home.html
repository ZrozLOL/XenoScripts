What's going on here?

XenoScripts is a growing Telegram community dedicated to sharing high-quality Roblox scripts. We post updates regularly — new scripts, patches for broken ones, and general announcements.

Everything is free. No paywalls, no required payments. Most scripts are keyless so you can copy and run in seconds on any supported executor.

We're still small but growing. The goal is to build the cleanest, most organized script hub on Telegram.
